Notifying other people identified under Regulation 11(1)(c)

Regulation 11(1)(c) of the Infrastructure Planning (Environmental Impact Assessment) Regulations 2017 (opens in new tab) (opens in a new tab) requires the Secretary of State or relevant authority to notify you of any people they consider:

  • likely to be affected by, or have an interest in, the project, or
  • unlikely to become aware of the project by the standard public notice

You must send a copy of the notice to any such people.

Have you notified any other people under Regulation 11(1)(c) about the project?
