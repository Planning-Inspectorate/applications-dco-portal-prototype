Environmental statement

You must submit an environmental statement where the project requires an environmental impact assessment (EIA). An EIA identifies and assesses the significant effects likely to arise from a project.

To see if the project requires an EIA and what you must include in an environmental statement, you should refer to The Infrastructure Planning (Environmental Impact Assessment) Regulations 2017 (opens in new tab) (opens in a new tab).
