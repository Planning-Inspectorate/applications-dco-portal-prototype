Habitat Regulations Assessment (HRA) documents

This could include:

  • a short statement saying the development won’t affect any European or Ramsar sites
  • a No Significant Effects Report (NSER), if screening shows it’s very unlikely to have any significant effects on these sites
  • an HRA Report, if screening shows it might have significant effects

The HRA Report should also include information for the derogation tests, if needed.
